A Little About Me...

My name is Megan Lasersohn, and I am currently a student at the College of Wooster. This website is a showcase of photos from some of the different places that I have traveled throughout my life, as well as where I currently live. All photos posted on this site were taken by me or my good friend Sofia Jain. You can find more of Sofia's work on Instagram!
